instructions:
1. click on the "game" button to play a dress-up game !!
2. when playing the game, you must create at least 4 gingerbreads. at least 4 gingerbreads should wear a different option from one of the main categories (eyes, mouth, hair, glasses, hats).
** for example, 4 of your gingerbreads could wear a different hat so that all the hat options were chosen !!

rules:
1. choose a pair of eyes for your avatar.
2. choose a mouth for your avatar.
3. choose a hair for your avatar.
4. choose a pair of glasses for your avatar.
5. choose a hat for your avatar.
6. hit the "restart" button and repeat this process at least 3 more times !!
